# Freight Operations Expertise

This skill provides comprehensive knowledge of freight forwarding operations, shipping modes, equipment, documentation, and logistics workflows.

## Shipping Modes

### Ocean Freight

**Full Container Load (FCL)**
- Shipper uses entire container exclusively
- Door-to-door control of cargo
- Lower per-unit cost for large shipments
- Standard transit: 14-45 days depending on route

**Less than Container Load (LCL)**
- Cargo consolidated with other shippers
- Pay per CBM or weight (whichever greater)
- Additional handling at CFS (Container Freight Station)
- Suitable for <15 CBM shipments

**Mode Comparison:**
| Factor | FCL | LCL |
|--------|-----|-----|
| Minimum | 1 container | 1 CBM |
| Cost structure | Per container | Per CBM/weight |
| Transit time | Direct | +3-7 days for consolidation |
| Handling | Minimal | Multiple touchpoints |
| Damage risk | Lower | Higher |
| Best for | >15 CBM | <15 CBM |

### Air Freight

**Service Levels:**
| Service | Transit | Cost | Use Case |
|---------|---------|------|----------|
| Express | 1-3 days | $$$$$ | Urgent, high-value |
| Standard | 3-5 days | $$$ | Time-sensitive |
| Deferred | 5-7 days | $$ | Less urgent |
| Charter | Custom | $$$$$ | Oversized, project cargo |

**Air Freight Calculations:**
```
Chargeable Weight = MAX(Actual Weight, Volumetric Weight)
Volumetric Weight (air) = L × W × H (cm) / 6,000
```

**Dimensional Factor:** 6,000 cm³/kg (industry standard)

### Intermodal / Multimodal

- **Rail**: Cost-effective for inland transport (US, China, EU)
- **Truck**: First/last mile, regional distribution
- **Barge**: River transport in EU, China

**Common Combinations:**
- Ocean + Rail (mini-landbridge)
- Ocean + Truck (door delivery)
- Air + Truck (express delivery)

## Shipping Documentation

### Bill of Lading (B/L)

**Types:**
| Type | Negotiability | Use |
|------|---------------|-----|
| Original B/L | Negotiable | L/C transactions, title transfer |
| Telex Release | Non-negotiable | Faster release, trusted parties |
| Sea Waybill | Non-negotiable | Same shipper/consignee entity |
| Express B/L | Non-negotiable | Speed, no original required |

**B/L Functions:**
1. Receipt for goods
2. Contract of carriage
3. Document of title (if negotiable)

**Key B/L Fields:**
- Shipper / Consignee / Notify Party
- Vessel / Voyage / Port of Loading / Port of Discharge
- Container numbers and seal numbers
- Package count, description, weight
- Freight terms (Prepaid / Collect)

### Other Key Documents

| Document | Purpose | Issued By |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| Commercial Invoice | Value declaration, sale terms | Exporter |
| Packing List | Detailed cargo contents | Exporter |
| Certificate of Origin | Country of manufacture | Chamber of Commerce |
| Dangerous Goods Declaration | Hazmat details | Shipper |
| Phytosanitary Certificate | Plant products | Government agency |
| Inspection Certificate | Quality verification | Third party (SGS, etc.) |

## Incoterms 2020

### Shipper-Friendly Terms

| Term | Risk Transfer | Seller Responsibility |
|------|---------------|----------------------|
| EXW | Ex Works (seller's premises) | Minimal - make goods available |
| FCA | Free Carrier (named place) | Deliver to carrier |
| FAS | Free Alongside Ship | Deliver alongside vessel |
| FOB | Free On Board | Load onto vessel |

### Buyer-Friendly Terms

| Term | Risk Transfer | Seller Responsibility |
|------|---------------|----------------------|
| CFR | Cost and Freight | Pay freight to destination port |
| CIF | Cost, Insurance, Freight | Pay freight + insurance |
| CPT | Carriage Paid To | Pay carriage to named place |
| CIP | Carriage, Insurance Paid | Pay carriage + insurance |
| DAP | Delivered at Place | Deliver to destination (unloaded) |
| DPU | Delivered at Place Unloaded | Deliver and unload |
| DDP | Delivered Duty Paid | Full delivery including duties |

### Incoterm Selection Guide

| Priority | Recommended Term |
|----------|------------------|
| Minimize risk (buyer) | DDP, DAP |
| Minimize risk (seller) | EXW, FCA |
| Balance risk/cost | FOB, CIF |
| Control shipping | FCA, CPT |

## Port Operations

### Terminal Activities

| Activity | Description | Typical Time |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| Vessel Berth | Ship arrives at dock | 0.5-2 days |
| Discharge | Unload containers | 1-3 days |
| Yard Storage | Container in terminal | Free time varies |
| Gate Out | Trucker picks up | Same day |

### Free Time & Demurrage

**Demurrage** (at terminal):
- Free time: Typically 3-7 days
- After free time: $50-200/day per container
- Increases after additional thresholds

**Detention** (equipment use):
- Free time: Typically 4-10 days
- After free time: $50-150/day per container
- Charged by carrier

**Per Diem** (chassis rental):
- Daily rental for container chassis
- Typically $20-50/day

### Port Congestion Factors

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Vessel bunching | Berth delays |
| Labor shortage | Slower operations |
| Chassis shortage | Pickup delays |
| Weather | Vessel/terminal delays |
| Peak season | All factors worse |
